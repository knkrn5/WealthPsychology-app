About Us

WHAT WE DO?

WealthPsychology, started in 2024, aims to demystify finance for the common person and promote widespread financial literacy. Our vision is to transform the education landscape in money management and personal finance, ensuring that everyone, regardless of their background, can make informed financial decisions. We believe that financial literacy is a crucial component of long-term economic stability and personal success. By breaking down complex financial concepts into easily understandable modules, we empower individuals to take control of their financial futures.

Our educational programs are divided into three comprehensive levels: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advance. At the Beginner level, we have covered Basics of the Stock Market, IPO(Initial Public Offering) and Mutual Funds. Intermediate we will delve into Fundamental and Technical Analysis, and in the Advanced level introduces advanced concepts like Futures and Options and upcoming concepts and more.

MY STORY

In 2021, my interest in the stock market was sparked by the web series "Scam 1992," which fueled my curiosity about finance and trading. I began closely following the stock market and sought to deepen my understanding of it. I learned how to conduct fundamental, technical, and economic analysis, and then delved into various financial assets, including Derivatives, cryptocurrencies etc, through practical experience.

Despite my academic background in computer applications (BCA), my passion has always been rooted in finance. This dual interest in finance and technology has driven me to combine my skills in both fields. As part of my ongoing learning process, I am currently building this website from scratch, utilizing my coding skills to create a platform that reflects my understanding of finance and stock market analysis. This project allows me to merge my interests in computer science and engineering with my dedication to financial analysis, providing a comprehensive learning experience that keeps me engaged and motivated.
