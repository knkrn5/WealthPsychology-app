Introduction

Welcome to the basics of the stock market. Here you will learn the foundational concepts that every investor should know.

Key Terms

  • Stock: A share in the ownership of a company.
  • Market Cap: The total value of a company's outstanding shares of stock.
  • Dividend: A portion of a company's earnings distributed to shareholders.
  • IPO: Initial Public Offering, the first time a company sells stock to the public.

How to Invest

Investing in the stock market involves researching companies, buying stocks, and managing your portfolio.

  1. Open a brokerage account.
  2. Research and select stocks.
  3. Place your stock orders.
  4. Monitor and manage your investments.
